Progressive Alliance UK

Never has their been a time when the future of our country has felt so uncertain. When our rights, communities and our collective future have been so at risk.

A progressive alliance of people, from all parties and none, need to forge a vision of what our post-'Brexit' relationship with the EU and the rest of the world looks like. Ensuring we can look forward to a positive, progressive future...

Petition for a Progressive Alliance

We call on the leaders, elected representatives, and members of progressive parties throughout the United Kingdom to form an alliance and work together to avert a worst case 'Brexit' scenario.

Petition for a General Election

We call upon Parliament to debate a motion of no confidence in the Government prior to Article 50 being triggered.

All political parties must have a chance to put forward their vision & negotiating position for the UK outside of the EU, such that members of the public can have a say on the type of 'Brexit' in a General Election.
