Petition for a Progressive Alliance

We call on the leaders, elected representatives, and members of progressive parties throughout the United Kingdom to form an alliance and work together to avert a worst case 'Brexit' scenario.

Petition for a General Election

We call upon Parliament to debate a motion of no confidence in the Government prior to Article 50 being triggered.

All political parties must have a chance to put forward their vision & negotiating position for the UK outside of the EU, such that members of the public can have a say on the type of 'Brexit' in a General Election.

Write to your MP

Writing to your MP is a good way to raise awareness of your concerns with them, and may be especially important & effective if you live in the constituency of a party leader, or senior member within a party.

Support a Progressive Alliance

If your MP is a member of the Labour Party, the Liberal Democrats, the Green Party, Plaid Cymru, or the Scottish National Party, call on them to support a progressive alliance of parties to avert a worst-case Brexit and deliver our aims.

Write to a member of the House of Lords

The House of Lords is not directly elected, so you do not have a specific member to represent you, but you can still write to any member of the Lords to make your view known. If we all write to a member of the House of Lords we could have a significant impact!

WriteToThem makes it easy to find a member of the Lords.

Support a Progressive Alliance

Write to a member of the Lords who belongs to the Labour Party, the Liberal Democrats, the Green Party, Plaid Cymru, or the Scottish National Party, and call on them to support a progressive alliance of parties to avert a worst-case Brexit and deliver our aims.
